journey as a designer

2011-2013

I started out by playing around with pixels just out of curiosity. I did over 70 freelance projects, mostly making websites and graphics. Design didn’t mean anything more than photoshop back then.

Summer 2014 - Zomato

Being from an engineering background, I landed my first tech internship at Zomato. Eventually became a product design intern where I really started to learn what design is all about. Read more about my experience.

Winter 2014 - Zo rooms

From a 3 person tech team to 20 people. It was one of the fastest environment I had ever worked at. Designed the website, iOS and Android apps and a game.

Summer 2015 - Google

This was my first opportunity to work in one of the world’s biggest tech company. I worked with Google Classroom and Forms team on different projects over 12 weeks in New York. Read more about my experience.

Present - Tablehero

Making seamless products for local businesses.

disclaimer

Over past few years, I have been extremely lucky to have worked with some of the best designers and engineers around the world. I have only learned things by doing and by seeing other people do. The work you see above is not entirely mine. It has gone through hours, days, weeks of discussions and collaboration with people who believe in building simple products solving complex problems with patience. I'm full of gratitude for these people for their invaluable lessons.
